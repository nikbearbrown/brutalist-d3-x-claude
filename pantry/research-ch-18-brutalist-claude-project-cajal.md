## 2. Core Concept — State of the Field

Scientific illustration sits between data visualization, explanatory diagramming, and publication design. The central research question is whether the figure makes mechanism, evidence, and uncertainty legible without overstating what the data shows.

## 3. Application Domain Examples

1. Mechanism figure with labeled biological process.
2. Multi-panel scientific figure combining chart, diagram, and annotation.
3. Interactive explainer for cellular or neural structures.
4. D3-driven figure export pipeline for manuscripts and slides.
5. Claude-assisted critique of figure clarity, hierarchy, and evidence claims.

## 4. Book's Thesis Connection

CAJAL is a strong project fit because it forces the human author to specify the visual claim. Claude can help implement panels and interactions, but the scientific argument must come from the author.

## 6. Pedagogical Delivery Research

## 7. Representation and Display Research
